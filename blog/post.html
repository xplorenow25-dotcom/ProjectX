How to Use Leverage Responsibly

Published on October 12, 2026 • 5 min read

The Double-Edged Sword

Leverage allows traders to borrow capital from an exchange to increase their position size. While this magnifies potential profits, it equally magnifies potential losses.

Calculating Your Margin

Before entering a highly leveraged trade, it is crucial to calculate your Initial Margin. Using our Futures Calculator, you can determine exactly how much of your own capital is locked up.

Pro Tip: Never use 100x leverage on highly volatile altcoins. Stick to lower leverage (2x-5x) until you have a proven, profitable strategy.

Conclusion

Always use strict stop-loss orders when trading on margin. By combining disciplined risk management with our calculation tools, you can protect your portfolio from sudden liquidations.
