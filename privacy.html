Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

CryptoMetricPro prioritizes your privacy. All calculator inputs are processed locally in your browser using JavaScript. We do not store, track, or transmit your financial data, entry prices, or portfolio sizes to any external servers.

2. Local Storage & Cookies

We use standard local storage (localStorage) exclusively to remember your preferred currency display settings (e.g., USD, EUR, GBP) and your cookie banner preferences to improve your user experience.

3. Contact Form Information

If you choose to use our Contact Form, we will temporarily collect your name and email address solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. This data is never sold to third parties.
